About Spice Garden

Established in 2005, Spice Garden has been Mumbai's most beloved Indian restaurant for over two decades. Our chefs bring authentic recipes from the royal kitchens of Hyderabad, the coastal homes of Chettinad and the dhabas of Punjab — all under one roof.

Every dish is prepared fresh to order using hand-ground masalas, whole spices and the finest seasonal ingredients. Our signature clay tandoor, fired to over 450°C, gives our breads and kebabs an unmistakable smoky character you simply cannot replicate at home.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes! We have an extensive vegetarian menu including Veg Biryani, Paneer Butter Masala, Shahi Paneer, Palak Paneer, Dal Tadka, Paneer Tikka and more. All vegetarian dishes are prepared in separate cookware.

How spicy is the food?

Our dishes are prepared at medium spice level by default. You can request mild, medium or hot when placing your order via the special instructions field at checkout.

What is your delivery area?

We deliver within a 10 km radius of our restaurant in South Mumbai. Delivery time is typically 25–40 minutes depending on your location and time of day.

Can I customise my order?

Absolutely. Use the "Special Instructions" box at checkout to mention any dietary requirements, spice preferences or ingredient omissions. Our kitchen will do its best to accommodate your request.

Do you use halal meat?

Yes, all our chicken and mutton are sourced from certified halal-approved suppliers.
