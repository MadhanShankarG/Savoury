About Dragon Wok

Founded in 2010, Dragon Wok brings the vibrant street food culture of China’s Sichuan and Cantonese provinces to the heart of Mumbai. Our chefs trained in Hong Kong and Chengdu, ensuring every dish respects the original technique — from wok-hei fried rice to hand-folded dumplings.

We use imported sauces, fresh produce and high-BTU commercial woks to achieve the authentic smoky “breath of the wok” flavour that separates real Chinese cooking from imitations.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the food authentically Chinese or Indo-Chinese?

We serve both! Our soups, dim sum and desserts follow authentic Cantonese and Sichuan recipes, while our Manchurian, Chilli Chicken and Schezwan dishes are crowd-favourite Indo-Chinese preparations.

Can I adjust the spice level?

Yes — use the Special Instructions box at checkout to specify mild, medium or extra-hot. Our default preparation is medium spice.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Several of our rice dishes and soups can be made gluten-free on request. Please mention your requirement in Special Instructions and our kitchen will substitute soy sauce with tamari.

What is your delivery radius?

We deliver within 8 km of our South Mumbai kitchen. Typical delivery time is 20–30 minutes.

Are the momos freshly made?

Absolutely. All momos and dumplings are hand-folded fresh daily and steamed or fried to order — never frozen.
