About Sweet Heaven

Sweet Heaven was born in 2009 from a passionate patissier’s dream: to create a space where Indian mithai tradition and European bakery artistry exist side by side. Our head pastry chef trained in Paris and our mithai master brings 30 years of experience from a Kolkata sweet shop — together they create Mumbai’s most beloved dessert destination.

We use no artificial colours or flavours. Our chocolates are sourced from Belgium, our dairy from local farms, and our spices from the Malabar coast. Every item is made fresh on the day of delivery — never stored and reheated.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customise a cake for a special occasion?

Yes! We offer custom cakes for birthdays, anniversaries and weddings. Place your order at least 48 hours in advance via our contact page or Special Instructions at checkout with your message and design preference.

Are your items egg-free?

Many items on our menu are eggless, including most Indian sweets, Gulab Jamun, Rasgulla, Besan Ladoo and our Kaju Katli. Each product page specifies eggless status. Simply ask in Special Instructions and we will confirm.

How long do the sweets stay fresh?

Indian sweets (Kaju Katli, Ladoo, Barfi) stay fresh for 5–7 days at room temperature. Cakes and pastries should be refrigerated and consumed within 2–3 days. Chocolates last 2 weeks in a cool, dry place.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes — free premium gift wrapping with a handwritten message card is available on all orders above ₹599. Simply write your message in the Special Instructions field at checkout.

Can I pre-order for a future date?

Absolutely! Use the Special Instructions field to mention your preferred delivery date and time window. We accept pre-orders up to 7 days in advance and will confirm via WhatsApp.
