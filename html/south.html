About South Treat

South Treat was founded in 2003 by a family from Bangalore, with a single mission: to bring the heartwarming, wholesome flavours of South India to every table in Mumbai. Every recipe is passed down through generations and cooked using traditional stone-ground batters, freshly grated coconut and sun-dried spices.

Our dosa batter is naturally fermented for 18 hours. Our sambar is slow-cooked for over two hours. And our filter coffee decoction is brewed fresh every 30 minutes throughout the day. That’s the South Treat promise.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the food purely vegetarian?

Most of our menu is vegetarian. We do serve Kerala Fish Curry and other seafood specials. Vegetarian dishes are clearly labelled and prepared in separate cookware.

Is your dosa batter freshly fermented?

Yes! Our rice and lentil batter is soaked, ground and naturally fermented for 18 hours. We prepare a fresh batch every morning. No preservatives are used.

Can I order a full South Indian meal plate (thali)?

Yes — our South Indian Thali (available on request in Special Instructions) includes rice, sambar, rasam, two vegetable curries, curd, papadam and a sweet for ₹249.

Do you use packaged coconut chutney?

Never. All our chutneys — coconut, tomato and peanut — are ground fresh every morning using real coconuts and traditional stone grinders.

Can I get filter coffee in a traditional tumbler and davara?

Absolutely! Just mention “Tumbler Davara Set” in Special Instructions and we will pack your filter coffee in a stainless-steel tumbler and davara for the authentic experience.
