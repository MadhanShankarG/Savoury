About Burger Point

Started by two burger-obsessed friends in 2012, Burger Point has grown from a tiny food truck to Mumbai’s most-loved smash burger joint. We source grass-fed beef from Maharashtra, hand-press each patty fresh to order, and bake our brioche buns in-house every morning.

Every element on our menu is crafted from scratch — the sauces, the pickles, the coleslaw, the batter. We don’t cut corners because great burgers deserve great ingredients.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the beef halal?

Yes, all our beef and chicken are sourced from certified halal-approved suppliers in Maharashtra.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan burgers?

We have the Crispy Veggie Burger made with a black bean and corn patty. For vegan customers, request no mayo in Special Instructions and we will substitute with our vegan sriracha sauce.

Can I order a burger without the bun?

Yes — request “lettuce wrap” or “no bun” in Special Instructions for a low-carb option. We will wrap your patty and toppings in crisp romaine leaves.

What sauces are available?

All orders come with ketchup and mustard. Additional sauces (chipotle, ranch, BBQ, honey mustard, hot sauce) are available as add-ons at ₹20 each.

Do you offer combo meals?

Absolutely! Each burger can be upgraded to a combo with any fries and a beverage for ₹150 extra. Select “Make it a Combo” in your order notes.
