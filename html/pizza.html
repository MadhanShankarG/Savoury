About Pizza Palace

Pizza Palace was born in 2008 from a simple belief: great Italian food should be accessible to everyone. Our head chef trained in Naples and Milan, mastering the art of Neapolitan pizza-making and traditional northern Italian pasta technique.

Our dough ferments for 48 hours before it reaches the wood-fired oven at 480°C, giving every base that perfect char, airy crust and chewy crumb. We import 00-flour, DOP San Marzano tomatoes and Fior di Latte mozzarella directly from Italy.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are the pizzas made in a wood-fired oven?

Yes! All our pizzas are baked in a 480°C gas-assisted wood-fired oven that replicates the authentic Neapolitan method, giving you that characteristic charred crust and leopard spotting.

Do you offer vegan options?

We have several vegan-friendly items including our Panzanella Salad, Penne Arrabbiata (without parmesan) and Rosemary Focaccia. Please mention vegan in Special Instructions at checkout.

Can I request a gluten-free base?

Yes, we offer a gluten-free pizza base for an additional ₹60. Please select this option in your order notes. Note that our kitchen handles gluten-containing flour, so trace cross-contamination is possible.

What size are your pizzas?

All our pizzas are 10-inch (25 cm) Neapolitan-style, which serves 1–2 people comfortably. We do not currently offer half-and-half topping options.

How do you keep the pizza crispy during delivery?

We use ventilated pizza boxes that allow steam to escape, keeping the crust crisp. We also recommend opening the box immediately upon arrival to let any residual steam out.
