About Savoury

Who We Are

Savoury was created with one simple goal : to make food ordering easy, reliable, and enjoyable. We connect food lovers with the best local restaurants and home kitchens, bringing delicious meals straight to your doorstep with just a few clicks.

Whether you're planning lunch at work, a family dinner, or satisfying a late-night craving, Savoury helps you discover quality food from trusted restaurants across the city.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to fresh, delicious food without compromising on convenience. Our mission is to provide fast delivery, exceptional customer service, and a wide selection of cuisines while supporting local restaurants and chefs.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become India's most trusted online food ordering platform by creating memorable dining experiences through technology, innovation, and outstanding service.

What We Offer

  • Fresh meals prepared by trusted restaurants.
  • Multiple cuisines including Indian, Chinese, Italian, Mexican and more.
  • Quick and reliable doorstep delivery.
  • Easy online ordering with secure payments.
  • Live order tracking from kitchen to doorstep.
  • Friendly customer support whenever you need assistance.
